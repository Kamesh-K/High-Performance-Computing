### Couette Flow

##### Controlling Equation for the Couette Flow:

$$
u_{i,j}^{t+1} = u_{i,j}^{t} +  \frac{\mu\Delta t}{\rho \Delta y^2 }(u_{i,j+1}^{t}  - 2u_{i,j}^{t+1} + u_{i,j-1}^{t}  )
$$

where:
$$
\mu = 0.0089 \\
\rho = 1000 \\
\Delta y = 0.1 \\
\Delta t = 1 \\ 
L_x = L_y = 10 \\
V_{Plate} = 5 \\
$$
